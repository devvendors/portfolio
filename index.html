Tearms & Conditions

For development services

We would like to bring to your attention that our company offers a range of reliable development services at competitive prices. Our payment process is transparent and secure. To initiate the project development, we require a deposit. Upon completion of the project, we provide a free trial period during which you can test the software at no cost. Should you require any modifications or encounter any issues during this trial period, please do not hesitate to contact us. Additionally, we are pleased to offer free domain and hosting services during the trial period. However, after the trial period expires, the software must be purchased, and domain and hosting services must be acquired and paid for.

Thank you for considering our services. Please let us know if you have any questions or would like to proceed with our development services.

For other services

If you're interested in our web security or marketing services, there's no need to make an advance payment. Simply contact us and we'll provide the service you need. If you prefer a monthly service, we may ask for an advance payment, but please don't worry as we always strive to be honest and transparent in our work.
